About us

Have you ever had the feeling that what you are paying for a service is way too much? Well, you are probably right. This feeling is at the core of our motivation and mission to create this marketplace for you as the consumer and service provider.

Our online marketplace provides the platform for consumers to find service providers at reasonable cost. We believe that the cost of any repair, service or installation should be fair. No other website provides this level of service and commitment to quality and low cost service as Xpertsbid. We want you to have the options to choose and decide your own service provider based on the critical details our platform provides, such as service provider reviews and qualifications.

Our unique bidding platform allows service providers to place bids for your projects. Xpertsbid philosophy of providing low cost service compels us to promote a marketplace where you can find the right service provider for your needs.

We found that many platforms on the web today provide quotes from preselected service providers. Our approach is different, we believe that providing quotes does not necessarily equate to quality or fair pricing. Our platform is a true bidding site that promotes competition between all service providers in each of the category we support.
